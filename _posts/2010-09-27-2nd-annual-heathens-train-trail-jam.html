The 2nd Annual Heathens Train Trail Jam is coming up on October 9th. It is making to out to be the best one yet. Lots of sponsors, all the lines are running smooth. Also there is going to be a hell of an after party at the Tommy T Compound right across the road with several bands playing and a huge fire.

Event Details
Main Event Train Comp
3 man teams / $20 a team member to enter. Judged from top to bottom on style, passing stunts, and costumes. All money goes to purse they want to hook up the top three teams. Last year we had about 4 teams i think each first place team rider each got $120! Not bad for just having some fun in the woods with some friends.

$2 to spectate or ride for day
$1 raffle tickets to win bulk prizes
All money goes to Train Comp Purse

James, Craig and Joe Oshman went on a little trip out to Ohio this past weekend.
Read more to check out the story and photos from the trip.

Not content with weekend tv shows or miserable central Pa weather, James H, Craig K and I packed up last Friday and ventured westbound. We were enticed by stories of crazy ramps and fun times and had to check out Ray’s MTB first hand. Neither Craig nor I have ever been to Ray’s and man were we blown out of our sneakers. The people behind Ray’s are either artists or mad scientist or both.

The session was a treat for all involved. Pump tracks, box rhythms, bowls, logs, 2X4’s everything got the shred and the shred was good. To sum things up; James rode like Coach Ditka driving daa Bears bus- total domination. The only thing about Craig that had more style than his riding is his holgan-stache, and I managed to pull off a few items of my own. All and all it was another awesome road trip with good pals and dang it that’s the best part of riding.

Keep your stick on the Ice –joe-0

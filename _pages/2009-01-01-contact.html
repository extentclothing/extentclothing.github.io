Where can I buy Extent Clothing?

You can purchase Extent Clothing from our online store, from one of us personally or by emailing and sending a money order to our address. Please make checks payable to "Bradley Hawkins."

how can i get stickers?

You can send a self-addressed stamped envelope with $1.00 ($3.00 if you are outside the United States) to Extent Clothing, 10942 Bailey Dr., Mill Creek, PA 17060. Or you can order some shirts online and get some that way too.

how can i get sponsored by extent clothing?

We do not sponsor riders based on e-mail request or sponsor me tapes. Everyone on our crew is someone we got to know and be friends with. So your best bet is to travel, meet people, and enjoy riding your bike and having fun with your friends because thats what its all about.
